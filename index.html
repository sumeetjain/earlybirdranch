Summer At Early Bird Ranch

We are excited to say that Summer Chickens are now available for pre-order. We can deliver to the following venues for your convenience: Pescadero Farmers Market, 8/3, 8/17, 9/1, 9/15, 5-8 pm, $3.95/pound fresh; and Flea Street Cafe Parking Lot (Menlo Park), 8/13, 8/27, 9/10, 9/24, 9-11am $4.95/pound frozen.

It is also the beginning of Turkey season here on the farm and we are officially taking pre-orders for Thanksgiving turkeys. Turkeys will be $4.95/pound and averaging 12-18 pounds each. We will have a limited number of fresh turkeys this year, but our frozen turkeys are NOT injected with saline, so they go to the table like a fresh bird! We are still determining pickup days, but all days will be scheduled so you can put your bird directly into your refrigerator. Please contact us to order soon--we sell out fast!

Email us for information about possible drops in San Mateo, Half Moon Bay, and Santa Cruz, or to pre-order pastured rabbit!
