Wondering how to buy chickens?

Saturday, June 4 and June 18, Menlo Park Delivery: From 9am-11am on Saturday, June 4 and Saturday, June 18 we will be delivering chickens to the parking lot of Flea Street Cafe in Menlo Park. These chickens will all be frozen, and will cost $4.95/pound. (If you order before Friday, June 3, we can cut the chicken up for $2.50 per chicken).

On Farm Pickup By Appointment: If you'd prefer to come to the farm, you can schedule an appointment for on-farm pickup. We're around most days, just let us know when you'd like to drop by. Chickens picked up on the farm will cost $3.95/pound. (If you order before Friday, June 3, we can cut the chicken up for $2.50 per chicken).

Happy Spring!

This season has certainly shaped up well, and we are truly enjoying hearing the wonderful reviews of our chicken from all of you!

We wanted to update you all and let you know that we are now experimenting with a weekly buying club drop in Menlo Park. The drop site is at Flea Street CafÈ (our favorite restaurant!) and will be held on at least the next two Saturdays (May 14 and May 21) from 9am-11am in their parking lot. The buying club drop is quite simple. We will process and freeze the birds a couple days before the drop, and then deliver pre-ordered birds to Flea Street CafÈ for you all to pick up during the two hour window.

While we still offer chickens for on-farm fresh pickup at $3.95 per pound or frozen for an extra $1.25 flat fee, the buying club rate will be $4.95 per pound to cover the costs of delivery and our absence from the farm. Either way we will be happy to accept payment by check or cash.

If you are interested in pre-ordering, please let us know as soon as possible via email at Info@EarlyBirdRanch.com or by calling ShaeLynn or myself (Kevin) at (619)855-9926.

Thank you so much for your support! Kevin and ShaeLynn
