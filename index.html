Early Bird Ranch in Pescadero is now taking pre-orders on our pasture-raised broiler chickens and turkeys.

Once out on pasture, our chickens live in low density populations and are protected from predators by 8 foot by 8 foot mobile pasture shelters that are moved every day at sunrise and mid-afternoon. This method ensures the chickens have twice-daily access to fresh and undisturbed pasture and means that their diet has a higher proportion of forage and bugs than free-ranging alone provides.

We supplement their access to pasture with feed that is free of antibiotics and hormones. We source our feed and chicks from California's Central Valley, and we are constantly striving to provide a product that is as local, nutrient-dense, and affordable as possible.
